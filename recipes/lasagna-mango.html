Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types, made of very wide, flat sheets.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Set a rack in the center of the oven, about 10 inches from the heat source.
  2. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  3. Add everything
  4. Cook for 5 mins
  5. Add garnish
